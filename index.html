About the Studio

Crafted with Passion, Worn with Pride

Gee's Klodin is a boutique sewing studio nestled in Dome, Accra — a creative hub where fabric, skill, and imagination come together. Founded with a deep love for tailoring and fashion design, we specialise in creating stunning custom garments for every occasion.

From breathtaking bridal gowns adorned with intricate beadwork to bold evening wear and elegant everyday pieces, every item we produce is a one-of-a-kind creation made to celebrate the person wearing it.

Beyond crafting beautiful clothes, we are passionate about passing on the art. Our studio welcomes both beginner learners and dedicated apprentices who want to build a career in fashion.

What We Offer

Our Services

From luxurious custom gowns to hands-on sewing lessons, Gee's Klodin offers a full range of fashion services for the community.

Bridal & Wedding Wear

We create breathtaking bridal gowns, bridesmaid dresses, and wedding party outfits — fully bespoke and tailored to your dream vision.

Custom Evening Wear

Gorgeous gowns and co-ords for parties, dinners, graduations, and every special occasion. Designed exclusively for you.

Everyday Fashion

Stylish everyday outfits — from casual chic to smart office wear — crafted from fabrics you choose and love.

Beading & Embroidery

Intricate hand and machine beadwork, stoning, and embroidery that adds that extra layer of magic to any garment.

Alterations & Repairs

Bring in any garment — we'll resize, repair, and restore it to a perfect fit with care and precision.

Sewing Lessons

Learn to sew at your own pace with our guided lessons for beginners and intermediate students. Classes available in the studio.

Come Join Our Studio

Whether you're a complete beginner or an aspiring fashion designer looking to sharpen your skills, Gee's Klodin welcomes you. We train with heart, discipline, and real-world experience.

Beginner Lessons
